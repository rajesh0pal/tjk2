Frequently Asked Questions

What are the age requirements for each class?

  • Playgroup: 1.8 years to 2.5 years
  • Nursery: 2.5 years to 3.5 years
  • LKG (Lower Kindergarten): 3.5 years to 4.5 years
  • UKG (Upper Kindergarten): 4.5 years to 5.5 years

Can you tell me more about the XSEED curriculum?

The XSEED curriculum is a comprehensive and proven learning program that replaces the traditional "tell-me" approach with a practical, hands-on method. It helps children develop critical thinking and problem-solving skills, preparing them for future success in school and in life.

How can I inquire about admissions?

You can contact us via email at info@thejourneykids.com or visit our Contact section on the homepage to find more details and schedule a visit.
